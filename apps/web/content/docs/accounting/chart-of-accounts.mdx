# Chart of accounts

| Code | Name | Role |
|------|------|------|
| 1000 | Cash on Hand | Cash |
| 1100 | Commercial Bank | Bank |
| 1200 | Card Clearing | Card |
| 1300 | Accounts Receivable | AR |
| 2100 | Accounts Payable | AP |
| 2150 | Goods Received Not Invoiced | Optional GRNI |
| 2200 | Output VAT | Sales tax liability |
| 2300 | Input VAT | Purchase tax asset |
| 3000 | Owner Equity | Equity / opening stock credit |
| 3100 | Owner Drawings | Drawings |
| 4000 | Sales Revenue | Revenue |
| 4100 | Sales Returns | Contra revenue |
| 5000 | COGS | Cost of sales |
| 5100 | Inventory | Inventory asset |
| 6000–6800 | Expenses | Operating expenses |

Products can override revenue/COGS/inventory codes on the product master.
