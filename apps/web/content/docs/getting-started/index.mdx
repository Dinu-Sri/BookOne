# Getting started

## Sign in

1. Open your BookOne URL.
2. Go to **Login** and sign in with your account.
3. After login you land on **Simple Entry** (home) or the last module you used.

If you see a login redirect loop, clear site cookies and try again.

## Main areas

| Area | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Simple Entry** | Quick money in/out, transfer, invoice/bill (no stock) |
| **Sales** | Quotes, orders, invoices, payments, returns, POS list |
| **Purchase** | PO, GRN, bills, cash purchase, pay vendors, aging |
| **Inventory** | Products, stock levels, ledger, transfers, adjustments |
| **Parties** | Customers and vendors |
| **Accounting** | Journal, transactions, accounts, reports, reconciliation |
| **Company** | Profile, tax, sales/purchase/inventory settings, brands, locations |
| **Control Room** | Super-admin modules & **ERP Health Check** (staging) |
| **Docs** | This documentation (`/docs`) |

## Recommended first-day setup

1. **Company → Details** — legal name, currency, timezone  
2. **Company → Tax** — TIN / VAT if needed  
3. **Company → Sales settings** — VAT rates, credit limit enforce (optional)  
4. **Company → Purchase settings** — approval, GRN rules, GRNI (optional)  
5. **Company → Locations** — warehouses if multi-site  
6. **Inventory → Products** — create physical products with cost & sell price  
7. **Parties** — at least one customer and one vendor  

Then try a full loop: **purchase stock → pay vendor → sales invoice → receive payment**.

## Staging vs production

- **Production** companies hold real books.  
- **Staging** companies can run **ERP Health Check** and **Master wipe**.  

Never mark a live company as Staging unless you accept full test data and wipe risk.
