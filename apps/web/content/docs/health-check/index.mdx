# ERP Health Check

**Control Room → ERP Health Check** (super admin).

## Purpose

Run a **mini business day** automatically and assert:

- Correct GL account mapping  
- Stock movements  
- Payment journals  
- Optional VAT, Simple Entry, credit limit, negative stock, POS, average cost, transfers  

## Steps

1. Mark company **Staging**.  
2. Turn **off** purchase bill approval (suite needs immediate post).  
3. **Run full suite** (or core only).  
4. Read pass/fail per step.  
5. Optionally **wipe this run** or use **Master wipe**.  

## Full suite highlights

- Purchase → pay vendor → sale → return → receive  
- GRN path without double stock  
- Tax invoice output VAT 2200  
- Simple Entry money out  
- Credit limit & negative stock **must block**  
- POS open sale **and close shift**  
- Weighted average cost  
- Multi-location transfer  

Numbers vary by **seed** so runs are not identical.
