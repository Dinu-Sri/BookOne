# Pay a vendor

## Steps

1. **Purchase → Payments → New**.  
2. Choose pay-from account (Cash/Bank).  
3. Allocate amounts to open bills.  
4. Submit.

## Accounting

- **Dr 2100** AP  
- **Cr** bank/cash  

Bill balances update to partial/paid.
