# Receive a customer payment

## Steps

1. Open **Sales → Payments → New** (or Payments list).  
2. Choose **payment date** and **deposit account** (Cash 1000, Bank 1100, Card 1200).  
3. Select open invoices and enter amounts (cannot exceed balance due).  
4. Submit.

## What posts

- **Dr** cash/bank account  
- **Cr 1300** Accounts Receivable  
- Invoice `paidAmount` / `balanceDue` update; status → partial or paid  
- A **settlement allocation** links payment to invoice transactions  

## Prefer this over Simple Entry

Simple Entry **customer payment** credits AR globally but does **not** allocate to invoice documents. Use **Sales → Payments** for proper subledger matching.
